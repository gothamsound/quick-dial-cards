TRANSMITTER Astral TX / HH (display) · Mini (app)
  1. 1Astral TX / HH: open the menu → set Country / Tuning Band first (unlocks the legal range), then Frequency.
  2. 2Dial the assigned MHz and confirm; turn RF on.
  3. 3Astral Mini (no screen): set frequency from the A20-Remote app, or push it from the receiver / Nexus over NexLink.
PORTABLE RECEIVER A20-RX (2-ch portable receiver)
  1. 1Press ◀ or ▶ to select a channel (single-channel view); press the centre ENTER to open its menu.
  2. 2Go to Frequency → ENTER; ◀/▶ to set the assigned MHz; ENTER to confirm. (An * just means it’s off the preset sub-channel grid — fine.)
  3. 3Set the transmitter to the same MHz (on-unit, app, or NexLink). Tip: AutoAssign scans a chosen band and fills both channels.
RF OFF FIRSTUse the menu RF Off toggle (or set RF Power = Off on the Mini) so it’s powered but not radiating; set the freq; then RF On.
RF POWERA20-TX 2 / 10 / 20 / 40 mW; Mini Off / 2 / 10 / 20 / 40; HH up to 100 mW (Extra-High must be enabled). Set on the unit, A20-Remote, or the receiver. Tip: raise Long-Range modulation before power.
MATCHING TX & RXNo IR sync. TX and RX must share the same frequency + modulation; match by hand, via A20-Remote, or push over NexLink (the screenless Mini must be set this way).
WATCH OUT
  • Set Country / Tuning Band first — available freqs depend on region and any FA license code; restricted bands stay locked.
  • GainForward: no gain on the transmitter — gain is set at the receiver / mixer.
  • Digital & intermod-immune, but keep A20 units ≥ 400 kHz apart.
